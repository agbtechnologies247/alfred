How to use A.L.F.R.E.D. to reduce OpEx

Step-by-step guide for your first 30 days

1

Connect your monitoring systems

Install the Datadog, Prometheus, or CloudWatch connector from the Marketplace. A.L.F.R.E.D. ingests telemetry — no alerts forwarding needed.

Datadog Connector Prometheus Connector AWS CloudWatch 
GET http://localhost:3000/api/marketplace/packages/connectors
→ Install conn-datadog or conn-prometheus
2

Deploy your first templates

Browse the template catalog and activate the highest-ROI templates for your environment. Start with Identity (password resets) — immediate payback, zero risk.

Recommended first templates (data from /api/opex/roi):
3

Run in copilot mode — approve AI decisions

All automated actions appear in the Decision Engine first. Before executing, A.L.F.R.E.D. runs a blast-radius simulation. Your team approves or rejects — and every decision trains the AI model (RLHF).

POST http://localhost:3000/api/decisions/simulate
{ "action_type": "restart_service", "target_entity_id": "orders-api" }

Response: { risk_score, affected_customers, estimated_downtime_minutes, alternatives }
4

Enable full autonomy for low-risk workflows

Once the AI confidence on a template exceeds your threshold (e.g. 95%) and the blast radius stays under your limit, enable auto-execute. Your SRE team is notified after, not before.

⚠ Keep Critical-severity templates in copilot mode until you have ≥30 feedback cycles. The system needs enough human decisions to trust itself.
5

Review your ROI monthly — from the live API

Run the ROI endpoint at any time to get current savings data. This feeds your board-level OpEx reduction report with no manual effort.

curl http://localhost:3000/api/opex/roi | jq .summary

{
  "template_count": 21,
  "total_monthly_occurrences": 404,
  "monthly_hours_saved": 87.4,
  "monthly_sre_savings_usd": 13110.0,
  "annual_sre_savings_usd": 157320.0,
  "weighted_avg_ai_confidence_pct": 96.4
}

The endpoint always reflects your current template catalog — no stale reports.

Methodology & Assumptions

What IS included in the savings figure

  • SRE/NOC engineer time freed per incident × hourly fully-loaded cost
  • Based on monthly_occurrences × estimated_resolution_mins per template
  • Weighted AI confidence factor NOT applied — assumes every execution is perfect (conservative)
  • Hourly cost default: $150/hr (Gartner 2024)

What is NOT included (additional upside)

  • Downtime avoidance (Gartner: $5,600/min for financial services)
  • SLA penalty avoidance
  • Security breach cost reduction (IBM 2024: avg $4.9M per credential compromise)
  • Compliance audit preparation time (estimated 3 weeks → 1 day with SOC2 template)
  • Staff re-allocation to higher-value engineering work

Data sources

  • Gartner (2024) — Fully-loaded IT staff cost benchmarks
  • IBM Cost of a Data Breach Report (2024)
  • Forrester TEI study — IT automation platforms (2023)
